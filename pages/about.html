Our Story

Rambling Odyssey Yoga started as an idea one morning at Amanda’s coffee table, as she enjoyed a warm mug of tea and worked through the flows she wanted to offer in her class that afternoon. Having worked in many different studios through the years, Amanda wanted to open her own studio in hopes of building a strong community who could encourage one another in deepening their yoga practices. With a grateful heart and a leap of faith, Amanda opened Rambling Odyssey Yoga in 2019 and hasn’t looked back. Come join our growing community of yogis and see what all the fuss is about!

Our Philosophy

Rambling Odyssey Yoga offers classes for new and experienced yogis alike; the hope is each student will find a way to connect with one another in an open, creative environment meant to broaden your yoga practice and deepen your personal experience. We are here to push ourselves mentally and physical, to encourage each other in our growth, and to find peace in the chaos of our daily lives. Our instructors are invested in helping their students discover their own limits and push past them. There’s a class for everyone at Rambling Odyssey Yoga and we can’t wait for you to join us.
